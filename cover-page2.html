Sofia AI

Live in production · 6 hotels
01 The Problem

Every hotel front desk closes. When it does, calls go to voicemail. A family landing at midnight needs a door code. A couple ready to book gets no answer. A tourist staring at a menu in a language they don't speak has no one to ask.

Every unanswered moment is a booking lost, a guest frustrated, a review that could have been five stars.

The chatbots that exist today don't solve this. They answer FAQs. They can't check real availability, pick up a phone call, or tell a guest which street to turn on. They talk. They don't act.

02 The Solution

Sofia acts. She picks up real phone calls through a custom SIP bridge. She knows who's calling before they say a word, matching the number to active reservations, greeting the guest by name, in their language.

She checks live availability, builds a quotation with a payment link, and sends it over WhatsApp the moment the call ends.

Hand her a camera and she sees what the guest sees. Point at a menu, she translates it. Point at a building, she identifies it. She knows where the guest is standing and gives directions from that exact spot.

Five channels. 23 live tools. Every response grounded in real API data. She never invents a price.

03 The Impact
  • Zero missed calls. Every after-hours call answered, understood, and acted on.
  • Direct revenue. Real quotations with payment links. Bookings that would have been lost to voicemail.
  • Live in production. Serving six hotels today. Not a prototype.
  • Staff time recovered. Repetitive questions handled. Staff focus on guests in front of them.
  • No language barrier at 2am. Automatic detection across Italian, English, French, German, Spanish.
  • Measurable. Every quotation tracked through creation, click, and booking with channel attribution.
